Main Character List

Mare Barrow

The protagonist and narrator of the novel, Mare Barrow is a seventeen-year-old Red (meaning her blood is red instead of silver) who has grown up in poverty in the poor village of the Stilts.She is not the best student or a talented artist like her sister so she makes her way by stealing to provide for her family. That is until one day she meets a kind stranger who instead of getting angry by her trying to pick pocket him; helps her by providing her a servant job at the Summertime Hall (the palace). That is until she find out she is more like the silves that she thought.

Cal (Tiberias VII) Calore

Tiberias Calore the Seventh, better known as Cal, or the kind stranger Mare met is the crown prince of Norta. He is a Silver with the power to control heat and flames. As the eldest son, Cal stands to inherit the throne his silver father created. Mare is drawn to Cal’s loyalty and sense of duty, which leads him both to do good deeds for her and to try to get to know his subjects. However, she is disgusted when his loyalty leads him to deploy the military tactics in which he has been well-educated.

Maven Calore

Maven Calore is Cal's younger brother who is also betrothed to Mare after the king and queen learn of Mare's power. Maven is surly at times and reveals that he is upset that he did not get to choose his wife, which is standard for a second-born son. Although Maven often acts immature because he is bitter that Cal gets all the privileges of a first-born prince, Mare identifies with his jealousy because of her own jealousy of Gisa (her talented younger sister), who her mother favors. Maven further impresses Mare by revealing that he is working with the Scarlet Guard and fighting for Red rights, and she begins to trust him.

Evangeline Samos

Cal’s fiancé. Evangeline is a ruthless Silver who can manipulate metal. She constantly tries to provoke Mare, and Mare worries that Evangeline is plotting against her.
